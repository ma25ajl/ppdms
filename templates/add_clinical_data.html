How Clinical Data Works

Clinical data is stored separately from patient identifiers:

  • Privacy Protected: Only pseudonym ID links to patient
  • Researchers: Can analyze clinical patterns without identifying patients
  • Clinicians: Need pseudonym ID to add/update records
  • Administrators: Can re-identify if medically necessary

Data Protection

All clinical data is:

  • Stored in pseudonymised format
  • Accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Audit logged for accountability
  • GDPR compliant

Important Notice

Never include identifiable information in clinical data fields.

Avoid: names, addresses, phone numbers, NHS numbers, or any information that could identify a patient.
